Make a Post & Rung Stool

In this two days class, each student will build a post & rung stool with a weaved seat. Students will learn various aspects of traditional chair making such as timber selection, shaping stool parts on a shave horse with drawknife and spokeshave, mortise and tenon joinery, grain orientation for joinery and glueing up with hot hide glue.

This will be an intensive class that places a heavy emphasis on hand tool work. Students can expect a certain level of labour required. The hard work results in a beautiful, sturdy and comfortable stool that can last.

To keep learning as effective as possible, there will only be a maximum of three students in a class.

All required tools and materials will be provided. Terms & Conditions apply, please read.
