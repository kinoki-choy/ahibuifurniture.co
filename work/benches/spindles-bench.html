Spindles Bench

Designed to be a luxurious single seater, this bench can also be made longer to accommodate more seaters and any height as required.

The versatility makes it suitable to be used as a dining bench, settee or as an occasional seating.

Bench pictured is in American White Ash. Finished with Rubio Monocoat hardwax oil.

L34" x W14" x H34"
L863mm x W356mm x H863mm
