Reamer Bench

The split stretcher draws inspiration from a chairmaker’s tool; a reamer that reams tapered mortises. More than an aesthetic feature, the new form of stretcher retains its original functionality in keeping the overall joineries robust.

Bench pictured is in Figured Sugar Maple. Live edge seat tops are subjected to availability.

L41" x W13" x H17¾"
L1040mm x W330mm x H450mm
