Vuc Audio Desk

A commissioned audio desk for the Director of Sound and Music, Digipen Institue of Technology. The audio desk comes with a pair of matching audio equipment racks as well as a keyboard tray made from Oak and Cherry. The spindles on the rack sides helps to aid ventilation for the audio equipment as well as to provide support for the top where it needs to take the weight of heavy speakers.

White Ash, White Oak & Black Cherry

L72" x W34" x H28"
L1828mm x W864mm x H711mm
