Mason Armrest Stool

The armrest stool is built upon the design of vernacular Appalachian Post & Rung chair/stool. By introducing angles into the posts(legs), it helps to reduce the boxy geometry. The rattan weaved seat, delicate yet strong adds a touch of finesse to the overall.

Stool pictured is in Angsana finished with Rubio Monocoat hardwax oil.

L19" x W17" x H25"
L483mm x W432mm x H635mm
